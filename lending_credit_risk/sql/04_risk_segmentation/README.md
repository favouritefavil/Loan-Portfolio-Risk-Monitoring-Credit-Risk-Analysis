# 04 — Risk Segmentation

## Objective
Identify which borrower segments carry the highest default rates and where portfolio exposure is most concentrated.

## Queries Included
- Default rate by credit grade (with portfolio share and total exposure)
- Default rate by DTI risk band
- Default rate by income group (with portfolio share and total exposure)
- Default rate by employment length group
- Default rate by loan purpose (with portfolio share and total exposure)
- Total exposure by DTI risk band

## Why It Matters
Headline default rates hide where risk is actually concentrated. These queries segment the portfolio across five borrower dimensions — grade, leverage, income, employment, and loan purpose — to identify the specific segments driving above-average default rates and the largest share of portfolio exposure. This is the analytical foundation for all dashboard visuals on the Risk Driver Analysis page.
